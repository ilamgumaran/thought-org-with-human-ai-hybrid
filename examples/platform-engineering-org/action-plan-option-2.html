4. Phase 0: Seed (Weeks 1–3)

Goal: Full baseline across all 3 layers, leadership alignment, pioneer selection, measurement infrastructure started.

Week 1: Baseline + Leadership

Baseline capture — execute all 3D actions in parallel

Leadership session (2 hrs) — HIO as experiment, three-layer measurement model ("adding outcomes and fulfillment, not replacing what we track"), 6-month timeline with phase gates, success/stop criteria

Week 2: All-Hands + Pioneer Selection

All-hands (1 hr) — purpose, transparency, baseline data shared. Even non-pioneer teams benefit from better metrics.

Pioneer selection (6-8 volunteers) — curious about AI (7+ readiness), low identity grip, mixed roles/experience, one constructive skeptic.

Week 3: Kickoff + Measurement Infra

Purpose workshop (half-day) — refine mission, map fulfillment, set bandwidth goals.

Measurement infrastructure (parallel) — DORA dashboard, platform metrics tracking, fulfillment pulse automation, comparison board template.

Key difference from Option 1: Measurement infrastructure starts Week 3, not Week 21. You can't prove anything you can't measure.

Exit Criteria

  • Complete baseline report — all three layers
  • DORA dashboard operational
  • Leadership aligned with phase gates
  • 6-8 volunteers selected
  • Measurement infrastructure started

6. Phase 2: Prove & Expand (Weeks 11–18)

Goal: Prove with data. Form 2 more units. Train coaches. Proving and expanding happen simultaneously.

Week 11: Deep Retrospective

Full-day: review ALL metrics across all three layers. Codify Playbook v1. Capture growth stories. Build the Comparison Board.

Weeks 11-14: Expand to 3 Units

Unit 2: Volunteers + 2 embedded pioneer coaches (3-week rotation)

Unit 3: Include constructive skeptics — tests model robustness

Remaining teams: Gradient adoption — harmony check, AI pre-analysis, fulfillment pulse, DORA tracking (good practice for everyone)

Weeks 14-18: The Comparison Board

Visible to the entire org. Full comparison across all metric layers:

LayerKey Metrics Compared
CurrentStories, velocity, merge rate
DORADeploy frequency, lead time, CFR, MTTR, classification
PlatformAsk→experiment, self-service, downstream NPS, adoption
Code HealthRework, defect escape, coverage, build time, tech debt
Dev ExperienceFocus time, friction, DX score, review turnaround
FulfillmentChallenge, purpose, growth, eNPS
InnovationNew capabilities, new/maintenance ratio, ideas
HarmonizationEmergence, AI sophistication, bandwidth
Show everything. Don't cherry-pick. Credibility comes from honesty.

Exit Criteria

  • 3 cognitive units operating
  • 5+ outcome metrics improved, no current metric degradation
  • DORA improved across the board
  • 3+ downstream teams report better experience
  • Fulfillment sustained at 7+
  • 3-4 trained HIO coaches
  • Leadership approves full transformation

  • Full metric review — all layers
  • Trend analysis and baseline comparison
  • DORA classification check
  • Playbook update

Quarterly: Evolution Review (Week 13 & 26)

  • Purpose still passing four tests?
  • Right unit composition?
  • New AI capabilities shifting balance?
  • Revise the action plan itself
Observe → What's actually happening? (all 3 metric layers + felt experience) ↓ Orient → What does it mean? ↓ Decide → What should change? (small bet, not big reorg) ↓ Act → Try it for one sprint ↓ Observe → Did it work?

9. Risk Management

Leadership Patience

Metrics dip earlySet expectations: 15% dip OK. DORA improvements show in 4-6 weeks — lead with those
"When ROI?"Monthly comparison board. DORA and DX improvements are immediate proof

Pioneer Burnout

Too much changeAI agents 2/week, practices incremental
Performance pressureFrame as learning. Reduce sprint 1-2 scope

6-Month Timeline Too Aggressive

Phase needs more timeExit criteria > dates. Extend phase, don't force
Too many units at onceStagger Unit 2 and 3 by 2 weeks
Phase 3 rushedCan extend beyond Week 26 — targets aspirational, criteria mandatory

Identity Grip

Role resistanceInvitational, not forced. Small expansions first
AI anxietyIdentity conversation using HIO Part II
Expertise devalued"Your judgment is MORE valuable — you evaluate AI's suggestions"
